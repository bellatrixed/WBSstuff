About Me

My name is Eowyn, and I am an American living in Germany.

I moved here to pursue my career as an opera singer, but life happens, and I find myself at a crossroads. I've decided to stay in Germany because I met my partner and we are planning on getting married in the spring, or maybe next fall. We just got a kitten named Oberon, and he's the littlest fairy king.

I live in a town outside of Leipzig, which is known as the pearl of the Mulde valley. It's really beautiful here, and I love going cycling through the countryside. I read fantasy novels, and I've been doing a lot of graphic design lately: I'm considering opening my own etsy shop to sell Christmas cards and the like.

About Creativity

The reason my partner thought I would take to code, is that web design has been one of my side gigs for years. I was designing for other singers with WYSIWG editors, (all of which I have an opinion on), as well as programs, logos, posters and other illustrations. I love organizing information visually, and I love beautifully organized information.

I started designing websites because, well, have you seen the average opera singer's website? It's awful.
I felt it was my duty to try and fix some of this, because it just.. was... not.. good. It made me so crazy.

But most importantly, I have a burning need to create. There is this energy that is always bursting to come out of me, be it in music, or visually when I design, or even through coding. I am driven to always be putting something new into the world. Coding has proven to be a wonderful outlet for that drive, as frustrating as it can sometimes be.

I am enamoured with the magic that you can do with CSS, without even adding JavaScript (I will admit that I am a little intimidated by JavaScript) I love finding a beautiful solution to a mundane problem (ask me about my personal account program project).
